West Virginia

February 25, 2017

Is your business in need of extra storage space? Do you need a place to keep your car in the winter? We manufacture and build quality steel storage buildings in West Virginia. Our buildings are engineer-certified and come with great warranties. You won’t be disappointed with your American Steel Carport purchase.

Commercial Street Buildings

Customers who run businesses need plenty of storage space to keep inventory and supplies. Our custom steel buildings are great solution to storage problems. Every building can be customized to your needs and is delivered to your property and assembled for you!

Steel Carports

West Virginia winters can be hard on your car’s exterior. Our steel carports are a great way to keep the ice and snow off of your car during the colder months. Carports come in a variety of sizes and styles to suit your budget and needs.
