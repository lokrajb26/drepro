About Us

The company was registered in 2010 under the Company’s Act

The company was awarded its first works for Technical and Operation Management of a Cutter Suction Dredger in 2011

Since then the Company has carried out several similar works

The Company has recently forayed into Inland Dredging works and will be procuring a cutter suction dredger.
