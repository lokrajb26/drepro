About Us

Dredgepro Solutions is registered under the Indian Company’s Act and commenced business in 2010

The company was awarded its first major works for Technical and Operation Management of a Cutter Suction Dredger in 2011

Since then the it has carried out several similar works

Dredgepro Solutions has recently forayed into Inland Dredging works and has procured a dismountable cutter suction dredger for undertaking works in Inland areas.

It has recently been awarded dredging works in an Inland Port in Africa.
